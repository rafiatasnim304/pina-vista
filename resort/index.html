Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about our investment opportunity

What is the minimum investment amount?
The minimum investment is for 1 share, priced at BDT 1,50,000. You can invest in multiples of 1 share.
How are dividends paid?
Dividends are paid quarterly based on the resort's performance. Expected annual returns are between 20% and 25%.
What are the investor benefits?
Investors enjoy quarterly dividends, discounts on room bookings, free stays (for multiple shares), and discounts at partner restaurants.
When will the resort be operational?
The resort is currently under construction and is expected to open from January ,2027. The project duration is 15 years (2026–2040).
